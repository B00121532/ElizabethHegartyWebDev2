    • Experiences

    Over the last year we have done & seen many different things

    September 2018- January 2019- We had peer mentoring in the university which gave us an opportunity to speak to our mentors if we had any problems, it also gave us time to speak to other people in our course and have lunch together.

    October 2018- We were invited to go to MAKE 2018 which was in Draiocht in Blanchardstown.

    October 2018- We visited The Huge Lane art gallery & the gallery of photography in Dublin City Centre.

    January 2019- We started working with Colm Keegan an Irish Poet which is helping us express ourselves and create poetry.

    February 2019- We visited Dell in Cherrywood Dublin, here we listened to people speak about disabilities in the workplace, it showed us where we could go for help if we have a disability and we are struggling to find work.

    March 2019- Some of us got the amazing oppertuinity to meet The Taoiseach Leo Varadkar on campus
